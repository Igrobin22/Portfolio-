What I Offer
Services

Every frame is intentional. I combine technical precision with a deep artistic sensibility to create edits that don’t just look good — they feel right.

From raw footage to final delivery, I handle the full post-production pipeline so you can focus on what matters.

01
Videography
Professional on-location shooting for real estate, brands, and events. Cinematic footage captured with intention — every shot framed to tell your story.
02
Short-Form Editing
Reels, TikToks, YouTube Shorts. Fast-paced, platform-optimized cuts with captions, sound design, and transitions designed to stop the scroll.
03
Long-Form Editing
YouTube videos, brand films, documentaries, and real estate walkthroughs. Full narrative editing from raw footage to final delivery.
04
Graphic Design
Social media graphics, brand visuals, flyers, and marketing materials. Clean, bold designs that stay consistent with your brand identity.
05
Thumbnail Design
Eye-catching YouTube and social thumbnails that drive clicks. Bold typography, sharp composition, and scroll-stopping visuals.
06
Podcast Shoot & Edit
End-to-end podcast production — multi-camera shooting, audio cleanup, editing, and short-form clip creation for social.
07
Logo Animation
Bring your brand to life with a custom animated logo. Perfect for video intros, outros, and social content. Multiple formats.
08
Website Development
Clean, modern websites built to showcase your brand. Portfolio sites, landing pages, and business pages with smooth animations and mobile-first design.
